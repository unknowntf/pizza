Welcome to Pizza Hut Grenada

Serving the Spice Isle with the World's Best Pizza Since 2010

Our Story

Since opening our doors in Grenada, we've been proud to bring the iconic taste of Pizza Hut to the beautiful Spice Isle. Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve delicious, quality pizzas while creating memorable dining experiences for our valued customers.

Located in the heart of St. George's, our restaurant combines Pizza Hut's international standards with local Grenadian hospitality, creating a unique dining experience that celebrates both global flavors and Caribbean charm.

Our Values

Quality

We use only the finest ingredients, ensuring every pizza meets Pizza Hut's global standards of excellence.

Service

Our team is dedicated to providing warm, friendly service that makes every customer feel like family.

Community

We're proud to be part of the Grenadian community, supporting local events and initiatives.
